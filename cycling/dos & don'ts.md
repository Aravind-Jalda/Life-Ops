## ✅ Do’s of Cycling

* Always wear a helmet
* Start with short distances and gradually increase
* Maintain proper posture
* Stay hydrated before and after rides
* Follow traffic rules
* Warm up before cycling and stretch after
* Check brakes and tire pressure before every ride

---

## ❌ Don’ts of Cycling

* Don’t skip helmet (even for short rides)
* Don’t overtrain in the beginning
* Don’t ignore body pain (knees, back)
* Don’t ride with low tire pressure
* Don’t use headphones in traffic areas
* Don’t abruptly increase distance or speed
