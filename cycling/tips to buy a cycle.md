## 🚲 Tips to Buy a Cycle

### 1. Choose Based on Purpose

* City riding → Hybrid bike
* Long distance → Road bike
* Rough terrain → Mountain bike

---

### 2. Frame Size Matters

* Wrong size = discomfort + injury risk
* Always test ride before buying

---

### 3. Budget Smartly

* Don’t overspend as a beginner
* Focus on comfort and reliability first

---

### 4. Check Key Components

* Brakes (disc brakes preferred)
* Gears (smooth shifting)
* Suspension (optional based on usage)

---

### 5. Brand & Service

* Choose brands with good service support
* Spare parts availability is important
