## ✅ Do’s of Swimming

* Start with proper warm-up
* Practice breathing separately if needed
* Focus on technique over speed
* Stay relaxed in water (avoid panic)
* Maintain hygiene (shower before/after pool use)
* Learn basic floating first (very important)
* Practice regularly for improvement

---

## ❌ Don’ts of Swimming

* Don’t hold your breath too long
* Don’t panic in water
* Don’t skip learning basics (floating, breathing)
* Don’t overexert in the beginning
* Don’t swim immediately after heavy meals
* Don’t ignore safety rules in pool
