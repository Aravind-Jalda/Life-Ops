# 🏊 Swimming Basics

## 🧭 Introduction

Swimming is a full-body, low-impact exercise that improves strength, endurance, and mental relaxation. This document captures the fundamentals, techniques, and personal practices to build consistency and improve performance.

---

## ⚙️ Basic Setup

### 1. Body Position (Very Important)

* Keep body straight and horizontal in water
* Head slightly down, not lifted too high
* Maintain a relaxed posture

---

### 2. Breathing Technique

* Inhale through mouth (quick breath)
* Exhale slowly in water (through nose/mouth)
* Maintain rhythm with strokes

---

### 3. Basic Strokes (Beginner Focus)

* Freestyle (Front Crawl) → easiest and most common
* Breaststroke → slower but easier to control
* Backstroke → useful for relaxation

---

### 4. Essential Gear

* Swimwear (comfortable fit)
* Goggles (clear underwater vision)
* Swim cap (optional but recommended)
* Towel & water bottle
