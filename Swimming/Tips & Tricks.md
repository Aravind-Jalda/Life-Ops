## 🏊 Tips & Tricks (Beginner Friendly)

### 1. Learn Floating First

* Floating builds confidence in water
* Makes learning strokes easier

---

### 2. Master Breathing

* Most beginners struggle here
* Practice breathing separately

---

### 3. Use Short Practice Sessions

* 20–30 minutes is enough initially
* Focus on quality, not duration

---

### 4. Break Learning into Steps

* Floating → kicking → breathing → full stroke

---

### 5. Stay Consistent

* 2–3 sessions per week gives visible improvement

---

## 🛒 Tips to Get Started (Buying & Preparation)

### 1. Choose the Right Pool

* Clean and well-maintained
* Proper safety measures
* Beginner-friendly environment

---

### 2. Invest in Basic Gear

* Good goggles (anti-fog recommended)
* Comfortable swimwear
* Avoid cheap gear that causes discomfort

---

### 3. Consider Coaching (Optional but Helpful)

* Helps correct mistakes early
* Speeds up learning
